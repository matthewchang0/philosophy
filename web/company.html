About Pantheon

Pantheon is a multi-model collaboration workspace built to help frontier models explore a question together, pressure-test ideas, and converge on the strongest answer they can produce.

What Pantheon is built for

Teams can use Pantheon for research, coding, strategy, writing, product work, and other open-ended tasks where multiple perspectives lead to better results than a single response alone.

How it works

Pantheon routes requests through secure server-side integrations, keeps conversation history tied to your account, and saves transcripts plus the final synthesis for later review.
